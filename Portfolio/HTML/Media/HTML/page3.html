Who Is Neil Young?

    Early Life -Activism and Charity- Top 3 Songs

1985

  1. Farm Aid
    1. Neil Young is a co-founder of Farm Aid, an annual benefit concert launched in 1985 to support family farmers in the United States. He has remained a passionate advocate for sustainable agriculture and often uses his performances to raise awareness about corporate influence in farming.

2006

  1. Living with War
    1. Neil Young’s 2006 album Living with War is a politically charged work that criticizes the Iraq War and the Bush administration. The album features raw, urgent rock music paired with protest lyrics, reflecting Young’s deep frustration with American foreign policy at the time.

2014

  1. Harvest The Hope
    1. Harvest the Hope was a benefit concert held in 2014 on a farm in Nebraska to protest the Keystone XL pipeline. Neil Young performed alongside Willie Nelson, using his music and platform to support environmental causes and indigenous rights.
